How it works: ARC Edu Search uses AI embeddings to understand the meaning of your query, prioritizing academic papers, educational resources, and authoritative sources from .edu, .gov, and .org domains.

Your privacy matters. No user tracking, no search profiles, no filter bubbles. Everyone sees the same results.
Search Syntax

"exact phrase" — Wrap text in quotes to require that phrase in the page title.
Example: best laptops "2026" — results must have "2026" in the title

<domain> — Wrap a domain in angle brackets to limit results to that site.
Example: <reddit.com> mechanical keyboards — only results from reddit.com

About
ARC Edu Search is an academic search engine that uses AI embeddings to understand meaning, not just match keywords.

No AI summaries. Unlike other search engines, we don't run expensive language model inference on every search. This saves energy and reduces our environmental footprint while giving you direct access to source pages.

Your privacy matters. No user tracking, no search profiles, no filter bubbles. Everyone sees the same results.
